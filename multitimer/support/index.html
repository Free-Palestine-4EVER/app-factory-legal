Frequently asked

Can I run more than one timer at once? Yes — that's the whole idea. Start as many named timers as you need ("Pasta", "Sauce", "Rest the dough") and each one counts down independently with its own ring, its own color, and its own alert.

My timer didn't alert me. Make sure Notifications are enabled for Multitimer in Settings → Notifications on your device — Multitimer uses local notifications to alert you when a timer finishes, even if the app is in the background, and each running timer schedules its own notification.

Does Multitimer work offline? Timers, the unit converter, favorites, and your shopping list always work fully offline. Recipes work offline too once you've opened one — it's cached on your device, including its photo.

Where do recipes come from? Recipe data is provided by TheMealDB, a free, open recipe database.

Is there an account or subscription? No. Multitimer is completely free, with no account, no ads, and no in-app purchases.
