Frequently asked questions

Does the app work offline? Yes — every core feature (the word game, word of the day, flashcards, streaks, favorites) works fully offline after the first launch. An internet connection only enhances definitions and related-word lookups when available.

Does the app require an account? No. There is no login and no account of any kind.

How does scoring work? Each round scores speed + word difficulty, with a combo multiplier that grows the longer your correct-answer streak runs within a session. Your daily best and all-time personal best are tracked per game mode.

What are the two game modes? Anagram Solve — unscramble a shuffled word's letters before time runs out. Synonym/Antonym Speed-Match — tap the correct synonym or antonym among four rapid-fire options.

Where does word content come from? A curated, offline word bank bundled with the app (including a dedicated ~3,100-word anagram puzzle bank), optionally enriched at definition-lookup time by the free Free Dictionary API and Datamuse services.
