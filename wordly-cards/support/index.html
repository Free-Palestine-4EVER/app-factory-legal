Frequently asked questions

Does the app work offline? Yes — every core feature (flashcards, word of the day, word game, streaks, favorites) works fully offline after the first launch. An internet connection only enhances definitions when available.

Does the app require an account? No. There is no login and no account of any kind.

How does the spaced-repetition scheduling work? Each flashcard tracks its own review history. Cards you consistently know move to longer review intervals; cards you're still learning come back sooner. The "Due Today" queue on the Flashcards tab always shows what's ready to review right now.

Where does word content come from? A curated, offline word bank bundled with the app, optionally enriched at definition-lookup time by the free Free Dictionary API and Datamuse services.
