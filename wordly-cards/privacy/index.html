Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Wordly Cards: Vocabulary ("the app") is built to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, and the app does not require an account, login, or sign-up of any kind.

What the app does NOT do

  • No account creation, login, or personal identifiers of any kind.
  • No advertising and no ad tracking (no IDFA, no ad SDKs).
  • No analytics SDKs and no usage tracking sent to us or any third party.
  • No access to your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera.

Data stored on your device

Your flashcard study progress, favorites, streak history, game scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only (using standard iOS local storage). This data is never transmitted anywhere. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Network requests

The app ships with a complete, offline word bank and cached definitions, so all core features (flashcards, word of the day, word game, streaks, favorites) work fully offline. When your device has an internet connection, the app may make anonymous, non-personal requests to two free, third-party dictionary services to refresh or enrich word definitions and related words:

  • Free Dictionary API (api.dictionaryapi.dev) — looks up a word's definition, phonetic spelling, and pronunciation audio. Only the word itself is sent; no personal or device identifier is included.
  • Datamuse (api.datamuse.com) — looks up related words, synonyms, and antonyms. Only the word itself is sent; no personal or device identifier is included.

These requests do not include any information that identifies you personally. We do not control these third-party services' own infrastructure-level logging (e.g. standard web server access logs), but no personal data is intentionally sent to them by this app.

Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
