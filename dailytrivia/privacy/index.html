PRIVACY POLICY

Daily Trivia Challenge

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Daily Trivia Challenge collects no personal data whatsoever. There is no account, no sign-up, no login, no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no third-party tracking of any kind embedded in this app.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers.

Data stored on your device

All gameplay data — your streak, scores, answer history, and settings — is generated by your device and stored locally on your device only, using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults and the Documents directory). This data is never transmitted anywhere, never leaves your device, and is deleted the moment you delete the app or use the in-app "Clear Stats" option.

Network requests

The app ships with a large offline question bank and works fully offline after installation. If you choose to tap "Refresh more questions" in Settings, the app makes a direct, anonymous request to the public Open Trivia Database (opentdb.com) to fetch additional trivia questions. No personal or device-identifying information is included in that request, and it is never made automatically or without your action.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder in Settings, iOS will ask for notification permission. This is used solely to show a local, on-device reminder — no data is collected or sent anywhere as a result.

Third-party content

Trivia questions are sourced from the community-maintained Open Trivia Database (opentdb.com), released under a Creative Commons license. No personal data is exchanged with this or any other third party.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind from any user, it is safe for all ages, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
