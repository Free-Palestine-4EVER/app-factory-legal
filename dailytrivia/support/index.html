Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app need internet access?
No. Daily Trivia Challenge ships with a large offline question bank and works fully in airplane mode from the very first launch, forever.

Why is my Daily Challenge streak reset?
The streak counts consecutive calendar days on which you completed that day's Daily Challenge. Missing a day resets the streak to zero the next time you play — this happens entirely on your device.

Is my data ever uploaded anywhere?
No — see our Privacy Policy. Everything is stored locally on your device only.

How do I clear my stats?
Open Settings → Clear Stats, and confirm. This immediately and permanently erases all local progress.

Where do the trivia questions come from?
The bundled question bank and any optional refresh come from the community-maintained Open Trivia Database.
