Frequently Asked Questions

Does Flagdom work without internet?
Yes — the Flag Quiz, Country Explorer, and Capital Quiz all use flags and country data bundled with the app and work fully offline. The only feature that needs a network connection is the live currency converter.

How current is the country data?
Flags, capitals, population, and currency data are refreshed from open public datasets (mledoze/countries, the World Bank, flagcdn.com) at build time and bundled into the app.

Why do exchange rates need internet?
Currency conversion uses live daily rates from a free public exchange-rate API; without a connection it falls back to the last bundled rates, clearly labeled as offline.

Is there an account or login?
No. Flagdom has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking — your quiz scores and streaks stay on your device.
