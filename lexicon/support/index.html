Frequently Asked Questions

Does Lexicon work without internet?
Yes — the Word of the Day, Flashcards, and Word Game all use a word list, definitions, and game bank bundled with the app and work fully offline. When you're online, the app quietly enriches entries with live definitions, pronunciation audio, and synonyms/antonyms.

Where do the definitions come from?
Word meanings, phonetics, and example sentences come from the bundled word list and, when online, the free Free Dictionary API. Synonyms, antonyms, and related words come from Datamuse. Both are free, open, public services.

How does the flashcard scheduling work?
Flashcards use a spaced-repetition algorithm — cards you know well are shown less often, and cards you're still learning come back sooner. Your "Due Today" queue updates automatically.

Is there an account or login?
No. Lexicon has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking — your streak, favorites, and scores stay on your device.

How do I reset my streak or progress?
Progress is stored locally. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears all local data, including your streak and flashcard progress.
