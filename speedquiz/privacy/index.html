Privacy Policy

Speedquiz: Fast Trivia

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Speedquiz: Fast Trivia is a timed trivia game (Speed Round, Daily Challenge, and Category browsing). This policy explains, plainly, what data it handles.

Data we collect

None. Speedquiz does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers. There is no account, no sign-in, and no tracking of any kind.

Network requests

The trivia questions used in the Speed Round, Daily Challenge, and Category Select modes are sourced from the free, public, keyless Open Trivia Database API and bundled with the app at build time — several thousand questions across roughly two dozen categories ship inside the app itself, so gameplay works fully offline from first launch. The only live network calls the app can ever make are an optional, user-initiated "refresh more questions" action in Settings, which fetches additional questions from the same Open Trivia Database API to top up the local bank. That request contains no personal information — just category and difficulty filters — and is never required for the app to function.

Data stored on your device

Your scores, best combo, questions-per-minute, streaks, accuracy stats, and settings are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data is never uploaded anywhere, is not visible to us, and stays on your device until you delete the app or clear it yourself in Settings.

Notifications

If you turn on the optional daily-reminder notification in Settings, the app asks the operating system for local-notification permission at that moment. No data leaves your device to enable this — it is a purely on-device, local notification.

Children's privacy

Speedquiz does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
