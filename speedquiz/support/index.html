Frequently Asked Questions

Does Speedquiz work without internet?
Yes — the Speed Round, Daily Challenge, and Category Select modes all draw from a large trivia bank bundled with the app and work fully offline. The only optional feature that needs a network connection is "refresh more questions" in Settings.

Where do the questions come from?
All questions come from the free, public Open Trivia Database, bundled into the app at build time across roughly two dozen categories.

How does Speed Round scoring work?
Answer as many questions as you can before the 30/60/90-second timer runs out. Consecutive correct answers build a combo multiplier that boosts your score; a wrong answer resets the combo. Your end-of-round summary shows score, best combo, and questions-per-minute.

Is the Daily Challenge the same for everyone?
Yes — everyone in the world gets the same 10 questions on a given calendar date.

Is there an account or login?
No. Speedquiz has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking — your stats and streaks stay on your device.
