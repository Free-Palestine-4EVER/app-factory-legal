Privacy Policy

Streaky: Habit Tracker

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Streaky is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

Data we collect

None. Streaky does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers. There is no account, no sign-in, and no tracking of any kind.

Network requests

Streaky makes zero network requests of any kind. There is no server, no API, no third-party SDK, no analytics library, and no crash reporter anywhere in the app. Every habit you create, every check-off, every focus session, and every sleep or water log is generated and stored only by you, on your device.

Data stored on your device

Your habits, streaks, focus session history, sleep logs, and water logs are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (SwiftData). This data is never uploaded anywhere, is not visible to us, and stays on your device until you delete the app or clear it yourself.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Streaky schedules local notifications on your device for focus-session completion, hydration reminders, and a bedtime wind-down nudge. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device via Apple's notification system — nothing is sent to any server.

Ambient sounds

The optional ambient focus sounds (rainfall, soft static, deep hum, night drone) are generated procedurally on your device in real time. No audio file is downloaded, streamed, or licensed from any third party.

Children's privacy

Streaky does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
