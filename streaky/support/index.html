Frequently asked

Does Streaky work offline? Yes — Streaky is 100% offline, always. There is no account, no server, and no internet connection required for any feature.

Where do my habits and logs live? Only on your device. Nothing is backed up to a server, because Streaky doesn't have one.

Is there an account or subscription? No. Streaky is completely free, with no account, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

My reminder didn't fire. Make sure Notifications are enabled for Streaky in Settings → Notifications on your device.

How do streak milestones work? Streaky celebrates 7, 30, 100, and 365-day streaks on any habit with a full-screen animation — keep checking off daily (or on your chosen cadence) to reach one.
