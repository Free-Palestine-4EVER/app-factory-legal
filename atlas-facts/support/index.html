Frequently Asked Questions

Does Atlas Facts work without internet?
Yes — every country's facts, flag, and quiz content are bundled with the app and work fully offline. The only feature that needs a network connection is the live currency converter.

How current is the country data?
Population, currency, and border data are refreshed from open public datasets (mledoze/countries, the World Bank) at build time and bundled into the app.

Why do exchange rates need internet?
Currency conversion uses live daily rates from a free public exchange-rate API; without a connection it falls back to the last rates fetched.
