Privacy Policy

Restwell: Sleep & Water

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Restwell is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

Data we collect

None. Restwell does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers. There is no account, no sign-in, and no tracking of any kind.

Network requests

Restwell makes zero network requests of any kind. There is no server, no API, no third-party SDK, no analytics library, and no crash reporter anywhere in the app. Every sleep log, water log, habit check-off, and focus session is generated and stored only by you, on your device.

Data stored on your device

Your sleep logs, water logs, habits, streaks, and focus session history are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (SwiftData). This data is never uploaded anywhere, is not visible to us, and stays on your device until you delete the app or clear it yourself.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Restwell schedules local notifications on your device for gentle hydration reminders, a calm bedtime wind-down nudge, and focus-session completion. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device via Apple's notification system — nothing is sent to any server.

Ambient sounds & chimes

The optional ambient focus sounds (rainfall, soft static, deep hum, night drone) and the session-complete chimes are all generated procedurally on your device in real time. No audio file is downloaded, streamed, or licensed from any third party.

Children's privacy

Restwell does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
