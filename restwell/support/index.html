Frequently asked

Does Restwell work offline? Yes — Restwell is 100% offline, always. There is no account, no server, and no internet connection required for any feature.

Where do my sleep and water logs live? Only on your device. Nothing is backed up to a server, because Restwell doesn't have one.

Is there an account or subscription? No. Restwell is completely free, with no account, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

My reminder didn't fire, or the wording seemed to repeat. Make sure Notifications are enabled for Restwell in Settings → Notifications on your device. Restwell schedules its hydration and bedtime reminders in gently-worded batches ahead of time — reopen the app occasionally to keep the queue topped up.

Can I change how often I get hydration reminders, or my bedtime? Yes — Settings → Reminders lets you turn each on or off and set the cadence and time.

Do the ambient focus sounds keep playing when my screen locks? Yes — Restwell is set up for background audio, so an ambient sound you start during a focus session keeps playing behind a locked screen.
