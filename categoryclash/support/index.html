Frequently asked

Does Category Clash: Trivia work offline? Yes — the app ships with a large bundled question bank across all ~24 categories, so Categories, Daily Challenge, and Speed Round all work fully offline, indefinitely. An internet connection is only used, optionally, to refresh or top up questions.

Where do my stats and mastery rings live? Only on your device. Nothing is backed up to a server, because Category Clash: Trivia doesn't have one.

Is there an account or subscription? No. Category Clash: Trivia is completely free, with no account, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

How does mastery work? Each category tile shows a progress ring based on your lifetime accuracy in that category — answer more questions in a topic to refine it.

How does the Daily Challenge work? Everyone in the world gets the same 10 questions on a given calendar date. It resets at midnight, and completing it keeps your daily streak going.

Where do the questions come from? The free, open Open Trivia Database (opentdb.com) — attribution is in Settings → Credits.
