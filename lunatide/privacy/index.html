Luna Tide: Moon & Sky

Privacy Policy — last updated July 17, 2026
Data Not Collected. No account, no analytics, no ads, no tracking.

Summary

Luna Tide is a moon-phase, sky, weather, and air-quality app. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. There is no user account, no sign-up, no advertising SDK, and no analytics SDK of any kind embedded in the app.

Location

Luna Tide uses your device's location (via Apple's CoreLocation, only with your permission) to show weather, air quality, moon phase, day length, and golden-hour times for where you are. Your coordinates are sent only to the public weather APIs described below, solely to fetch the forecast for that spot — they are not linked to your identity, not stored on any server we control, and not shared with any third party for advertising or tracking. You can also search for or select any city manually instead of granting location access — every feature still works.

Network Requests

Luna Tide fetches current conditions, hourly/daily forecasts, air quality, and (for coastal locations) sea-level and wave data from Open-Meteo's free, keyless public APIs, and resolves city search via Open-Meteo's geocoding API. These requests contain only latitude/longitude or a search string — no name, device identifier, or account data of any kind, because none exists in the app. Every successful response is cached on your device so the app still shows your last-known data (with an "as of" time) when you're offline.

Moon Phase, Day Length & Golden Hour — Fully Offline

The Sky tab's moon-phase visualizer, illumination percentage, full/new moon countdowns, day-length tracking, and golden/blue-hour windows are computed entirely on your device using standard astronomical formulas. No network request of any kind is made to produce this data.

Data Not Collected

In App Store terms: Luna Tide's data collection practice is Data Not Collected. We do not collect any data that is linked to your identity, and we do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children

Luna Tide contains no user-generated content, chat, or social features, and is safe for all ages.
