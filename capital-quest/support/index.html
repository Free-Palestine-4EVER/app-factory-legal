Frequently Asked Questions

Does Capital Quest work without internet?
Yes — the Capital Quiz, Flag Quiz, and Country Explorer are all bundled with the app and work fully offline, with no loading spinners. The only feature that needs a network connection is the live currency converter.

How are the difficulty tiers decided?
Easy, Medium, and Hard are computed from each country's real population and region data bundled in the app — Hard is weighted toward lower-population, lesser-known countries and territories, never hand-picked.

How current is the country data?
Capitals, population, currency, and border data are refreshed from open public datasets (mledoze/countries, the World Bank) at build time and bundled into the app.

Why do exchange rates need internet?
Currency conversion uses live daily rates from a free public exchange-rate API; without a connection it falls back to the last bundled rates, clearly labeled as offline.
