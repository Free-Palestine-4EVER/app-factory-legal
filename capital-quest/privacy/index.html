Privacy Policy

Capital Quest: City Quiz

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Capital Quest is a capital-city map-pin quiz and country explorer app. This policy explains, plainly, what data it handles.

Data we collect

None. Capital Quest does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers. There is no account, no sign-in, and no tracking of any kind.

Network requests

Country facts, capitals, flags, populations, and borders are bundled with the app or were fetched once at build time from free, public, keyless data sources — the open-source mledoze/countries dataset, the World Bank Open Data API, and flagcdn.com for flag images — none of which receive any information about you. The Capital Quiz, Flag Quiz, and Country Explorer never make a network call at runtime; they run entirely from data bundled inside the app. The one live feature, currency conversion, makes an anonymous request to the free exchangerate-api.com service for current exchange rates; this request contains no personal information, just the currency codes involved, and falls back to a bundled offline rate table if there is no connection.

Data stored on your device

Your quiz history, per-tier streaks, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data is never uploaded anywhere, is not visible to us, and stays on your device until you delete the app or clear it yourself in Settings.

Children's privacy

Capital Quest does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
